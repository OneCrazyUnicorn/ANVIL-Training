Training Timeline


  • Week 1 - Induction to ANVIL

    The aim of this manual is to develop you into an effective and efficient commercial and industrial property broker.


    We have encaptulated an exceptional amount of experience to create a step by step syllabus which provides a comprehensive background and understanding of the industry and will allow you to outperform a broker with years of experience.

  • Week 2 - Foundation

    The second week at ANVIL can be seen as a foundation phase, where you should be able to apply the theory and information gained in part 1 and encorporate the findings into building your database and understanding all the components which will lead you to becoming an area specialist.

  • Week 3 - Qualifying

    In your third week at ANVIL we focus on the process of successfully qualifying a lead from generating a brochure up to signing the lease.
